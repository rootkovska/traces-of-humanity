# About the blog and author

My name is Joanna Rutkowska and Traces Of Humanity is my new personal blog, launched in February 2026. My previous blog, [*Invisible Things*](https://blog.invisiblethings.org), has been retired since 2018. 

This blog is intended to be a reportage of my struggles between Rationality and Humanism. You can read more about in the [intro post](https://tracesofhumanity.org/hello-world/).

Also a place to discuss some of my projects :-)

## About Joanna

In the past I used to be a computer security researcher and architect, working on many projects over the span of around 20 years. Some of these projects include: Redpill, Bluepill, virtualization and x86 hardware vulnerabilities, Evil Maid (and Anti Evil Maid :p), Qubes OS, and Wildland. I also used to write a blog named [*Invisible Things*](https://blog.invisiblethings.org) about computer security and privacy.

Today I no longer do computer security. I do other stuff, part of which running this blog :-)
